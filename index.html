About EMatolweni Guesthouse

Welcome to EMatolweni Guesthouse, your premier destination for luxury accommodation in the heart of Kimberley, South Africa. Our guesthouse seamlessly blends African elegance with modern comfort, creating an unforgettable hospitality experience.

Whether you are visiting for business or pleasure, our dedicated team ensures your stay is comfortable, secure, and memorable, offering personalized service that reflects the warmth of African hospitality.

  • Centrally located in Kimberley
  • No Load Shedding (Mining Grid)
  • Water Tank Backup System
  • Secure Gated Property & ADT
  • Family-Owned & Managed
  • Budget-Friendly to Mid-Range
